Microlife Corporation

A Global leader in blood pressure monitors and digital thermometers

Overview

Microlife is one of the world leaders in the development and manufacturing of medical diagnostic equipment for home monitoring and institutional use. Blood pressure monitors, digital thermometers, asthma monitors and flexible heating are Microlife’s core business. Microlife has excellent R&D capabilities and Microlife products are sold in over 50 countries in Europe, the Americas and Asia. As the pioneer of the mercury-free digital thermometer, Microlife products are free of environmentally hazardous substances.
