China Medical University Hospital

Center for Reconstructive Surgery Reconstruction for Sever and Rare Deformities

Overview

Based on the outstanding capacity of reconstructive surgery with 17 supportive subspecialties, China Medical University has remarkable achievement in the reconstruction for patients with rare deformities. This center applying intra-abdominal organs for complex reconstruction under the collaboration of various subspecialties is even the top achievement of reconstructive surgery. Apply brand-new “Airborne Suture Technique” which accomplish the reconstruction which were impossible with conventional surgery. For example, “Voice Reconstruction with Autogenous Intestine”, was created by Dr. Hung-Chi Chen exclusively, which has made mute-patients regained their own voice.
more
In order to be successful in treating an individual case, this center clarifies the complex anatomic abnormalities, designs a detailed flow chart of procedures after discussion by all related subspecialties, reduce the risk of patient’s co-morbidities, good control of ischemia time of the harvested intra-abdominal organs, perform proper arrangement and insert for the transferred organs, provide specially designed physiotherapy programs for an individual patient. This center has become unique in providing integrated treatment by collaboration of various subspecialties. It successfully combines education, research and development of cutting edge techniques for the whole world.
